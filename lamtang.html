Trip Overview of Lamtang Trek

Langtang valley, located 61.8 km away from Kathmandu, amazes travelers with its mountain streams and stunning views. Mount Langtang Lirung (7246m) surrounds the rhododendron and pine forests that beautifies the valley. The warm hospitality and vibrant culture in the Langtang valley makes you feel like you are at home.

The Langtang trek starts from Syabrubesi (1460m) and reaches the highest point at Kang Jing Gompa at an altitude of 3900m. You can also opt to trek Kang Jing Ri, a day from Kang Jing Gompa, to see the mesmerizing 360-degree views of the Himalayas. The trek offers a route that ascends and descends at several places with spectacular views of landscapes, painted chortens, Buddhist prayers flags and much more.

During the trek, you can see the remains of the 2015 Gorkha earthquake. And see how the locals have restored back the trekking trails and the Langtang valley. This village is where you will see the live example of the rebuilding of the nation after the biggest tragedy. Langtang Valley Trek offers a majestic view of mountains with panaromic landscapes. It is among the third most visited trekking destinations in Nepal alongside Everest Base Camp Trek and Annapurna Base Camp Trek. Basically, this trek is exciting with picturesque views of the beautiful village that lies within the Langtang National Park. Langtang is the best vacation spot for regular and novice trekkers, and I am sure it will be your unforgettable lifetime experience.

    Lamtang Trekking Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival at Tribhuvan International airport, Kathmandu. We welcome you at the airport. Then, we will transfer you to the hotel by our car. Overnight at Hotel at Kathmandu.
  • Day 2: Drive from Kathmandu to Syabrubesi (Alt. 1467m/4813ft) by route bus. 145 km northwest of Kathmandu. Driving 6:30 hours. Overnight at Tea House.
  • Day 3: Trekking from Syabrubesi to Lama Hotel (Alt. 2470m/ 8104ft). Walking 5:60 hrs. Overnight at Tea House.
  • Day 4: Trekking from Lama Hotel to Langtang village (Alt. 3430m/11253ft). Walking 6 hours. Overnight at Tea House
  • Day 5: Trekking from Langtang Village to Kyanjin Gompa (Alt. 3870m/12697ft). Walking 3 hours. Overnight at Tea House.
  • Day 6: Hiking up Kyanjin Ri or Tsergo Ri (Alt. 5050m/16564ft) and back to Kyanjin Gompa. Walking 6 hours. Overnight at Tea House
  • Day 7: Trekking from Kyanjin Gompa to Lama Hotel (Alt. 2470m/ 8104ft). Walking 6 hours. Overnight at Tea House.
  • Day 8: Trekking from Lama Hotel to Syabrubesi (Alt. 1467m/4813ft). Walking 5 hours. Overnight at Tea House
  • Day 9: Drive from Syabrubesi to Kathmandu (Alt. 1350m/ 4429ft) by route bus. 145 km. Driving 6:30 hours.
  • Day 10: We drop you at Kathmandu International airport in our car for your flight to your country. Final Departure from Nepal..
